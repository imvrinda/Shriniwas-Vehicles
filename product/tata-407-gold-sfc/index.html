Tata 407 Gold SFC Overview

The Tata 407 Gold SFC is one of the best trucks in the 5T GVW category. It is able to carry high payloads across rural, semi-urban and urban roads, providing a promise of higher mileage and lower operating cost. The 407 Gold SFC is among the longest-running commercial vehicles in India, having been produced over the past 27 years.

Tata 407 Gold SFC Engine Capacity

In the BS6 mode, 407 Gold SFC gets the proven powertrain to be the most dependable business partner. It is fitted with Tata’s 4SPCR BS6 engine that produces a maximum power of 98hp and 300 Nm of torque, tuned to the 5-speed mechanical gearbox with synchromesh with 5-forward and 1-reserve gear.

Other features Tata 407 Gold SFC

The Tata 407 Gold SFC is a 4-wheeled truck regime, in the 4450 kg of GVW. Depending upon the variants you choose, the payload capacity ranges from between 2-and 2.4 tonnes. The 407 Gold is available in three variants, the cabin and chassis, high deck, and full side deck, with a stock wheelbase of 2955 mm, the gross vehicle weight is 4450 kg, and 40% degree ascending for steeper terrains.

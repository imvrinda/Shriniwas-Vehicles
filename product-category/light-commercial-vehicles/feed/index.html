Tata 610 SFC Overview

Shriniwas Vehicles’ light truck portfolio of SFC trucks is legendary, and the Tata Motors 610 SFC is based on the proven 407 platform that has been designed for light cargo and transport shipments. To offer you a value for money truck, Shriniwas Vehicles has optimized the powertrain with the BS6 engine and much more robust aggregates for superior reliability.

Tata 610 SFC Engine Capacity

The 610 SFC gets the proven 4SPCR, BS6 engine with 2956 cc of displacement. The maximum power of 98hp and 300 Nm of torque to take on all the cargo duties. The engine is tuned with Tata’s G400, 5-speed manual gearbox for easier driving and also better fuel mileage. 

Tata 610 SFC Price in India

This light truck is priced quite competitive in its category, you can purchase it in the price range of ₹ 13.68 – ₹ 14.09 lakh, ex-showroom, with customized cargo body options.

Other features Tata 610 SFC

The front suspension is a parabolic spring with a rubber bush and 2 hydraulic double-acting shock absorbers with an anti-roll bar.
